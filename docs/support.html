‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does plant identification work?

Simply take a clear photo of any plant and our AI will analyze it to provide instant identification. Make sure the plant is well-lit and the image is clear for best results.

How accurate is the health diagnosis?

Our AI provides highly accurate diagnosis based on visual analysis. However, for serious plant health issues, we recommend consulting with a local garden center or plant expert.

Can I use the app offline?

Plant identification and diagnosis require an internet connection to access our AI services. However, your saved plants and reminders work offline.

Is my plant data private?

Yes! Your plant data is stored securely on your device and our servers. We don't share your personal plant collection with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I set up care reminders?

After identifying a plant, the app automatically suggests care reminders. You can also manually create custom reminders for watering, fertilizing, and health checks.

Can I delete plants from my collection?

Yes! Go to "My Plants", select any plant, tap the menu button (three dots), and choose "Delete Plant" to remove it from your collection.

The app crashes or won't open

Try restarting your device and updating to the latest version of GardenHealth. If problems persist, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Send us an email with details about bugs or feature ideas. Your feedback helps make GardenHealth better for everyone.
